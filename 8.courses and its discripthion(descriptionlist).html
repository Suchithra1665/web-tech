MOHAN BABU UNIVERSITY

1.Computer Science Engineering(CSE):

This program focuses on the design, development, and testing of computer systems and software.

2.Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE):

this program deals with the design, development, and testing of electronic circuits, devices, and communication systems.

3.Mechanical Engineering (ME):

This program focuses on the design, development, and testing of mechanical systems, machines, and mechanisms.

4.Civil Engineering (CE):

This program deals with the design, development, and testing of infrastructure, buildings, and transportation systems.

5.Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE):

This program focuses on the design, development, and testing of electrical systems, circuits, and electronic devices.

6.Information Technology (IT):

This program deals with the design, development, and testing of software and hardware systems for information processing and management.

7.Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML):

This program focuses on the design, development, and testing of intelligent systems that can learn, reason, and interact with humans.

8.Data Science and Engineering (DSE):

This program deals with the design, development, and testing of systems that extract insights and knowledge from data.
