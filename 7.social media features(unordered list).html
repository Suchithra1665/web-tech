Social Media Features

Snapchat:

  • Ephemeral content(disappearing after 24 hours).
  • Stories for sharing behind the scenes content.
  • Lenses for filters for creative editing.
  • Snap map for location sharing.
  • Direct messaging for private conversations.

Pinterest:

  • Visual discovery and planning website.
  • Boards for saving and organizing content.
  • Pins for sharing images and videos.
  • Comments for engaging with pins.
  • Shopping features for purchasing products.

YouTube:

  • Video sharing and streaming.
  • Channels for creators and subscriptions.
  • Comments for community engagement.
  • Live streaming for real-time video.
  • Monetization options for creator.
LinkedIn:
  • Professional networking and resume building.
  • News feed for industry updates and articles.
  • Groups for networking and discussion.
  • Messaging for private connections.
  • Job searching and recruitment features.
Instagram:
  • Visual feed for sharing photos and videos.
  • Stories for ephemeral content.
  • Reels for short-form videos.
  • IGTV for longer-form videos.
  • Direct messaging for private conversations.
